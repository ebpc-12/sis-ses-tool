Please read each statement and indicate your most likely reaction — even if it depends on circumstances.

When you see words like "sexually aroused" or "aroused," these refer to feelings of sexual excitement, being stimulated, or "turned on."

There are no right or wrong answers. Please respond as honestly as you can.

Score Interpretation

Clinical Note: These results reflect self-reported tendencies and should be interpreted in the context of a therapeutic relationship.
